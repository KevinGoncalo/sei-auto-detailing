About SEI Auto Detailing

Built on Quality, Driven by Detail

My name is Joseph, and I’m the owner of SEI Auto Detailing, which stands for Superior Exterior Interior. I started this business with one goal in mind—to provide honest, high-quality detailing services that people can truly rely on.

For me, detailing isn’t just about making vehicles look clean. It’s about taking pride in the work, paying attention to the small details others overlook, and treating every vehicle like it’s my own. Whether it’s a daily driver, a work truck, or a vehicle someone takes special pride in, I approach every job with the same level of care and professionalism.

SEI Auto Detailing is a home-based and mobile service based in Warwick, RI and serving surrounding Rhode Island communities, which allows me to bring convenience and quality directly to my customers. I’m committed to using proper techniques and trusted products to safely clean, restore, and protect both the exterior and interior of every vehicle I work on.

Building this business means a lot to me, and my goal is simple: deliver Superior Exterior. Superior Interior. Superior Results.

Thank you for trusting SEI Auto Detailing.
